Chipotle Pinto Beans

Learn the secrets to making Chipotle-Style Pinto Beans in the comfort of your own kitchen. This copycat recipe starts with getting the right type of rice and cooking it in a non-traditional way. This rice is soft and fluffy with just the right amount of chew and no stickiness.

Ingredients

    Rice
    Cilantro
    Lime
    Salt
    Lemon
    Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Bring 8 cups of water to boil in a large pot.
  2. Once water has reached a boil, add rice , bay leaf, and salt to taste. Stir and return to boil.
  3. Boil uncovered for 10 to 12 minutes
  4. Stir in cilantro, lime juice, and lemon juice. Season to taste with salt.
  5. All done!
